# Reading Patterns

## Objective
Organize content according to common scanning behavior across devices.

## Patterns to support
- **F-pattern**: dense text pages, docs, and dashboards.
- **Z-pattern**: marketing and landing pages with clear CTA path.
- **Layered scan**: mobile users skim headings before details.

## Authoring rules
- Front-load key meaning in headings.
- Keep paragraphs short and scannable.
- Use bullets for comparisons and decisions.
- Place CTA where scan naturally lands.
