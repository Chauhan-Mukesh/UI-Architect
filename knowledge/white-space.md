# White Space

## Objective
Use space intentionally to improve readability, perceived quality, and focus.

## Guidelines
- Separate content by meaning, not decoration.
- Increase spacing around key actions to improve discoverability.
- Preserve rhythm with repeatable spacing scales.
- Prefer fewer sections with stronger separation over crowded layouts.

## Spacing behavior
- Tight spacing for related micro-content.
- Medium spacing for component boundaries.
- Large spacing for section transitions.
