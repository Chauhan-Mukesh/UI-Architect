# Portfolio Variant 02 — Long-Scroll Narrative

## Structure
- Intro section (position, current status).
- Sequential case-study sections, each with imagery + short story.
- About section woven in.
- Contact at the end.

## Use when
- The person's or studio's voice matters as much as the work.
- Fewer pieces (3–5) — each gets its own act.

## Grid & spacing
- Sections alternate between full-bleed imagery and constrained-copy blocks.
- Whitespace between sections is deliberate and generous.

## Breakpoints
- Mobile: preserve pacing; imagery stays large, copy stays readable.
- Desktop: allow bleeds, offsets, and editorial rhythm.

## Accessibility
- Anchor navigation optional; if present, jumps to sections labeled with headings.
- Motion in section reveals honors `prefers-reduced-motion`.

## Anti-patterns
- Autoplay everything.
- Missing case-study outcomes at the end of the story.
