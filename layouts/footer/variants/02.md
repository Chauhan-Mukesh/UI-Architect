# Footer Variant 02 — Structured Multi-Column

## Structure
- Top: 3–5 columns of grouped links (Product, Company, Resources, Legal).
- Second row: brand, address, social, locale switcher, newsletter (subtle).
- Bottom bar: copyright, ISO year, and micro-links (status, sitemap).

## Use when
- Marketing sites and SaaS with substantial secondary navigation.

## Grid & spacing
- Column headers styled distinctly (small caps or bold).
- Vertical rhythm consistent within columns.

## Breakpoints
- Mobile: columns collapse to accordion or stacked lists.
- Desktop: 3–5 columns.

## Accessibility
- Column headers use `<h2>` (visually appropriate size) or `<h3>` if nested.
- Newsletter form: real label, real button, clear success/error states.

## Anti-patterns
- 8 columns of dense links that no one reads.
- Newsletter form that hijacks the whole footer.
