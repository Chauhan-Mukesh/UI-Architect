# Footer Variant 01 — Compact

## Structure
- Single row: brand mark, 3–5 links, social icons, copyright.
- Legal links (privacy, terms) either in the row or on a second line.

## Use when
- Portfolio, small business, personal sites.

## Grid & spacing
- Height ~64–96px on desktop, wraps gracefully on smaller screens.
- Center or space-between layout.

## Breakpoints
- Mobile: content stacks; brand → links → social → copyright.
- Desktop: single row.

## Accessibility
- Social icons wrapped in links with accessible names.
- Copyright includes a stable year (or dynamic if maintained).

## Anti-patterns
- Legal links so small they fail 3:1 contrast.
- Hidden contact requiring a scroll into a modal.
