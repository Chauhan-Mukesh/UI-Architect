# Hero Variant 01 — Statement

## Structure
- Full-width surface, top-aligned nav.
- Single-column composition: eyebrow (optional) → headline → subhead → primary CTA (+ optional secondary text link).
- Optional understated trust strip (logos or one-line social proof) directly below the fold or inside the hero.

## Use when
- Positioning is verbal-first (SaaS, consulting, legal, agency thought pieces).
- The product does not benefit from an above-the-fold screenshot.

## Grid & spacing
- Content max-width 60–75ch centered or offset-left.
- Vertical rhythm: eyebrow (small caps), headline (display), subhead (body-lg), CTA. Generous vertical padding.

## Breakpoints
- Mobile ≤640: single column, left-aligned, headline scales to fluid `clamp(2rem, 6vw, 3.25rem)`.
- Tablet 641–1024: retain single column, wider max-width.
- Desktop ≥1025: same composition, more breathing space; no forced two-column split.

## Accessibility
- Headline is `<h1>`.
- Eyebrow is not styled as a heading; use `<p>` with visual small-caps.
- Focus ring on CTA maintains 3:1 contrast.

## Anti-patterns
- Adding a decorative right-side illustration to fill space.
- Two CTAs of equal weight.
