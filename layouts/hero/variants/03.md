# Hero Variant 03 — Immersive (Imagery-First)

## Structure
- Full-bleed image or short muted video as background, with overlay for readability.
- Overlay content: minimal — a short statement + primary CTA.
- Nav sits transparent over the image; changes on scroll.

## Use when
- Emotional or aesthetic communication carries the pitch (agency, restaurant, hotel, luxury, portfolio).
- The brand can survive without long explanatory copy in the hero.

## Grid & spacing
- Overlay content bottom-left or centered.
- Ensure overlay contrast: dark scrim ~40–60% opacity or gradient behind text.
- Reserve 30–50% of the surface for negative space around content.

## Breakpoints
- Mobile: image cropped focally, not letterboxed. CTA sits within safe area (respect notch/home indicator).
- Tablet: same treatment, larger content margins.
- Desktop: full-viewport height allowed; never longer than viewport.

## Accessibility
- Background video: no autoplay if it distracts from CTA; `prefers-reduced-motion` disables it.
- Ensure text-over-image contrast passes AA even at worst frame of a video.
- Provide a poster image with equivalent alt for video.

## Anti-patterns
- Autoplay video with sound.
- Text placed on the busiest part of the image.
- Overlay copy so short the visitor cannot orient.
