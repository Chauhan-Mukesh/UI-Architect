# Blog Variant 03 — Single Post Reading

## Structure
- Header: title, byline, date, read time, category, share.
- Optional hero image or figure.
- Body: prose with 65–75ch measure, images and pull quotes at wider bleed.
- End of article: author bio, next/prev, related posts.
- Optional in-page TOC for long-reads.

## Use when
- Long-form articles.
- Reader engagement matters (subscribes, shares).

## Grid & spacing
- Body copy centered, generous vertical rhythm.
- Images optionally break out to wider or full-bleed.
- Pull quotes styled to break the reading rhythm intentionally.

## Breakpoints
- Mobile: single column, no side content.
- Desktop: optional sticky TOC or share bar aligned to content.

## Accessibility
- Article wrapped in `<article>` with heading hierarchy.
- Images have meaningful alt.
- Share buttons are real buttons or links, not styled divs.

## Anti-patterns
- Sticky elements that block content on mobile.
- Reader-hostile modals mid-article.
