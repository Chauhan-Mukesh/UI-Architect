# Example — Portfolio

## Prompt
"Portfolio for an independent product designer who wants inbound roles at product-led companies."

## Research
- Audience: hiring managers at 50–500 person startups.
- Decision criteria: process depth, outcomes, taste.
- Journey risk: too many pieces dilutes the taste signal.

## Design direction
- Tone: personal, considered, precise.
- Layout: `layouts/portfolio/variants/01` (editorial grid).
- Type: `editorial` voice — serif display + humanist sans body.
- Colors: warm off-white background + ink text + one deep accent.
- Motion: minimal fades on scroll; reduced-motion respected.

## Output plan
- Intro line (name, discipline, one sentence).
- 5 case studies in a two-column asymmetric grid.
- Case-study detail pages with problem, role, process, outcome.
- About section (voice, working preferences).
- Contact: single email address.

## Review notes
- Case studies show outcomes with real numbers where possible.
- Thumbnails reflect the finished work, not lorem UI.
- No "click here" CTAs.
- Focus rings visible against every hero image.
