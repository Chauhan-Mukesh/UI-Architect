# Example — Medical

## Prompt
"Homepage for a mid-size specialty clinic (orthopedics)."

## Research
- Personas: prospective patient (self or family caregiver), referred patient, insurance verifier.
- Journey risks: patients bail if they can't verify insurance or find appointment availability.
- Trust signals: physician credentials, accreditations, patient safety ratings.

## Design direction
- Tone: calm, authoritative, accessible.
- Layout: hero variant 01 (statement) + landing variant 01 (classic conversion).
- Type: `medical` voice — humanist sans, slightly larger base.
- Colors: `industries/medical/recommended-colors.md` — clinical blues + off-white + warm accent for CTAs.
- Motion: minimal; reduced-motion respected.

## Output plan
- Hero: "Get back to what you love. Board-certified orthopedic care in [City]." + Book appointment CTA.
- Insurance / appointment strip near the fold.
- Providers list with photos and credentials.
- Services (conditions treated) with clear paths to detail pages.
- Trust: accreditations, safety ratings.
- FAQ with real patient questions.
- Location and hours with map.

## Review notes
- Contrast passes AAA on body text.
- Language target 8th-grade reading level.
- No dark patterns in appointment or intake flow.
- Emergency contact visible and clearly distinct.
