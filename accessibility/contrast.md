# Accessibility Contrast

## Goal
Ensure readable text and identifiable interactive states in all supported themes.

## Minimum thresholds
- Body text: 4.5:1
- Large text: 3:1
- UI components and focus indicators: 3:1 against adjacent colors

## Checks
- Evaluate default, hover, focus, disabled, and error states.
- Verify contrast in light/dark variants when provided.
- Ensure color is not sole carrier of information.
