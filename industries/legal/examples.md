# Legal — Example Direction

## Example page goals
- Help individual clients and business decision makers seeking legal help complete consultation requests and case qualification submissions with confidence.
- Keep one dominant action path with one support path.

## Suggested example scenario
- Build a conversion-focused page with hero, proof, process, FAQ, and closing CTA.
- Include constraints, assumptions, and mobile-first behavior notes.

## Review checklist
- Is the primary CTA clear in every stage?
- Is trust established before high-commitment actions?
- Are accessibility and clarity preserved across breakpoints?
