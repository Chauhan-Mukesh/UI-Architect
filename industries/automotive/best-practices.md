# Automotive — Best Practices

## UX priorities
- Design for vehicle buyers, service customers, and fleet decision makers with role-specific task paths.
- Keep key actions discoverable from the first viewport.
- Reduce ambiguity around commitment requirements.

## Content strategy
- Lead with capability and outcome clarity.
- Surface constraints and prerequisites early.
- Pair value claims with proof and operational details.

## Interaction guidance
- Keep one dominant CTA with contextual secondary support.
- Ensure forms and flows support correction without restart.
- Preserve accessibility in every interactive state.
