# Portfolio — Conversion Goals

## Primary conversion
- Target: qualified outreach (inbound roles, project inquiries, collaboration asks).
- Primary CTA: contact email or "Get in touch" (never both).

## Secondary conversion
- Follows on the platform where the audience actually engages (rarely a newsletter).
- Resume/PDF download for hiring workflows.

## KPI orientation
- Contact rate from unique visitors
- Case-study read-through
- Return visits (a signal a decision is being made)
