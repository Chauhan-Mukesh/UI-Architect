# Portfolio — Example Direction

## Example page goals
- Present selected work with enough context to earn a contact from the right audience.
- Convey personality without noise.

## Suggested example scenario
- Single-page portfolio: intro, 4 case studies, about, contact.
- Or multi-page: index + 3–4 case-study detail pages.

## Review checklist
- Would a stranger know what this person does within 5 seconds?
- Is the strongest piece first?
- Is contact obvious and singular?
