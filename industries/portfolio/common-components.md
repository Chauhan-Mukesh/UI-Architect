# Portfolio — Common Components

## Core components
- Introduction: name, discipline, current status
- Selected work grid or scroll gallery
- Individual case-study or project detail pages
- Short "about" with tone and specifics
- Now/currently module (optional, signals liveness)
- Simple contact block (email + relevant social)

## Component expectations
- Thumbnails must reflect the finished work, not lorem-styled previews.
- Case studies keep to the story; avoid corporate section templates.
- Contact is one CTA. Not a form and a Calendly and an email.
