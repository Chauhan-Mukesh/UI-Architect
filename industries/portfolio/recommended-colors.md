# Portfolio — Recommended Colors

## Palette direction
- Neutral base that lets work carry color. One personal accent for identity.
- Warm neutrals often feel more inviting than cool for solo portfolios.
- Dark or light mode both fine — pick one and commit.

## Usage constraints
- Palette must never compete with project thumbnails.
- Focus rings must clear WCAG 2.2 AA regardless of visual mood.
- Avoid trend palettes (glassmorphism, neon) unless they match the practitioner's actual voice.
