# Insurance — Best Practices

## UX priorities
- Build experiences for policy seekers, policyholders, and claim initiators with clear path segmentation.
- Keep top navigation focused on high-intent tasks.
- Reduce uncertainty before commitment decisions.

## Content strategy
- Surface critical details early (price, process, eligibility, terms).
- Balance persuasion with transparent constraints.
- Place proof directly adjacent to commitment actions.

## Interaction guidance
- Keep primary CTA prominent through the full journey.
- Provide clear state and progress feedback on multi-step flows.
- Ensure keyboard and screen-reader support for core transactions.
