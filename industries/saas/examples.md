# SaaS — Example Direction

## Example page goals
- Guide product evaluators, admins, and technical buyers to complete trial starts and qualified demo requests.
- Prioritize one dominant action path and one assisted path.

## Suggested example scenario
- Build a landing page with hero, proof, process, FAQ, and strong final CTA.
- Include copy constraints, trust evidence, and mobile-first behavior notes.

## Review checklist
- Is the primary CTA unmistakable?
- Is trust established before commitment?
- Are accessibility and clarity preserved across breakpoints?
