# Artificial Intelligence — Typography

## Typographic style
- Tone target: precise, calm, credible — the opposite of hype.
- Sans-serif body with a strong monospace for code and outputs.
- Numeric legibility matters (benchmarks, pricing, token counts) — favor tabular-nums.

## Readability rules
- Long-form safety and documentation content must remain 60–75ch.
- Do not letter-space benchmarks or numbers.
- Monospace pairs must remain readable on mobile — no ultra-condensed programming fonts.
