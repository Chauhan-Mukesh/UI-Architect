# Artificial Intelligence — Common Mistakes

- Vague benefit copy ("supercharge your workflow") without a concrete task.
- Demos that stall or return errors and hide it from the visitor.
- Benchmarks without methodology or cited comparisons.
- Missing or performative safety content.
- Pricing that hides usage units.
- Illustrations that anthropomorphize the model in misleading ways.

## Specific risk
- Presenting deterministic output when the system is probabilistic — sets false expectations and hurts activation.
