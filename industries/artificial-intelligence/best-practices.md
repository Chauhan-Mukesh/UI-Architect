# Artificial Intelligence — Best Practices

## UX priorities
- Design for evaluators comparing capability, cost, and safety — not for AI enthusiasts.
- Concrete outputs beat abstract benefit claims. Show what the system produces.
- Trust and honesty are the differentiators; overpromising damages the category.

## Content strategy
- Lead with the specific task the AI performs, not the underlying technology.
- Show real outputs, real prompts, real limits.
- Be explicit about what the system cannot do and where humans stay in the loop.

## Interaction guidance
- Interactive demos beat video demos. Let visitors try before signup where possible.
- Clearly separate marketing content from documentation and safety information.
- Cite model families, versions, and evaluation methodology where relevant.
