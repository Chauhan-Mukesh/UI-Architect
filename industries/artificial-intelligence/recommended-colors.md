# Artificial Intelligence — Recommended Colors

## Palette direction
- Restrained neutrals with a single confident accent; avoid the "AI purple gradient" cliche unless the brand owns it.
- Semantic state colors for success/warning/error must remain distinct from brand accent.
- Dark mode at parity for developer-facing surfaces.

## Usage constraints
- Do not use color to imply model confidence unless paired with a numeric signal.
- Maintain AA contrast on interactive demo surfaces (code, output, tooltips).
- Loading and streaming states use motion + color together, not color alone.
