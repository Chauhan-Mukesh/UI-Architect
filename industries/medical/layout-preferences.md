# Medical — Layout Preferences

## Recommended structure
1. Value proposition + primary action
2. Audience/use-case segmentation
3. Trust and proof block
4. Process or service steps
5. FAQ and risk reduction
6. Final conversion section

## Layout rules
- Keep section transitions obvious with spacing and headings.
- Use predictable reading flow and avoid decorative complexity.
- Ensure mobile hierarchy preserves CTA discoverability.
