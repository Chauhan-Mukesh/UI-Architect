# Restaurant — Conversion Goals

## Primary conversion
- Target: reservations made.
- Primary CTA: "Reserve a table" / "Book now".

## Secondary conversion
- Order online (if takeout/delivery in scope).
- Private events / catering inquiry.
- Directions and click-to-call.

## KPI orientation
- Reservation completion rate
- Click-to-call and directions taps (mobile)
- Menu views per session
- Repeat visits (proxy for local loyalty)
