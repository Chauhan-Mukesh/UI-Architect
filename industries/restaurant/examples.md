# Restaurant — Example Direction

## Example page goals
- Convey the room and food quickly; make reservations obvious.
- Cover the three intents (reserve, menu, location) without a full-page scroll.

## Suggested example scenario
- Single-page site: hero, facts strip, menu, reservation, gallery, story, location.
- Optional secondary pages for full menu, events, press.

## Review checklist
- Can a mobile visitor reserve in under 15 seconds?
- Is today's information (hours, closures) verifiable at a glance?
- Does the imagery match the actual experience or feel like stock?
