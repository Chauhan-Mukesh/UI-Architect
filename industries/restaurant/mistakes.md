# Restaurant — Common Mistakes

- Menu locked behind a PDF-only link.
- Reservation buried under a hamburger nav.
- Autoplay music or video that blocks the page.
- Stock photography that mismatches the actual restaurant.
- Missing or wrong hours / outdated seasonal content.
- Overly complex event/catering flows that overshadow reservations.

## Specific risk
- Missing structured data (`Restaurant`, `Menu`, `OpeningHours`) — hurts local discovery.
