# Restaurant — Recommended Colors

## Palette direction
- Warm, food-flattering neutrals (cream, bone, char) with a single deep accent.
- Match the room: candlelit bistros trend warmer; bright fast-casual trends cleaner and brighter.
- Avoid saturated blues in food-focused areas — they read as unappetizing near imagery.

## Usage constraints
- Ensure text over image overlays passes AA.
- Reservation CTAs should stand out visually from all other components.
- Do not sacrifice contrast for mood.
