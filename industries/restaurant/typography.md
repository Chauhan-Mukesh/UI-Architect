# Restaurant — Typography

## Typographic style
- Tone target: matches the restaurant's identity (elegant / warm / casual / bold).
- Display face signals the room; text face keeps menu and info scannable.
- Serif display + humanist sans body is a durable pairing for most restaurants.

## Readability rules
- Menu items must remain scannable in outdoor daylight on mobile.
- Prices and dietary info require clear hierarchy.
- Avoid ornamental scripts for anything longer than a headline.
