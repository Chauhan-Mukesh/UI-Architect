# Restaurant — Best Practices

## UX priorities
- Diners arrive with three intents: reserve, view menu, find location/hours. Serve all three above the fold.
- Photography carries most of the emotional persuasion; treat it as primary content.
- Mobile is dominant — most restaurant visits happen mid-decision, on the go.

## Content strategy
- Menu, hours, address, phone must be reachable in one tap.
- Voice reflects the restaurant's actual voice (fine dining, casual, neighborhood).
- Seasonal or special content stays current; nothing kills trust faster than a stale menu.

## Interaction guidance
- Reservation flow is a single, obvious CTA with a clear next step (widget or link).
- Do not gate menu behind a PDF unless print quality is essential.
- Click-to-call and open-in-maps on mobile — table stakes.
