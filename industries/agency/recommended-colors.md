# Agency — Recommended Colors

## Palette direction
- Editorial neutrals (paper, ink, off-black) that let work carry color.
- One assertive accent that signals confidence without decorating the page.
- Avoid full black/white default; commit to a specific temperature (warm or cool).

## Usage constraints
- Palette must not compete with case-study imagery.
- Maintain WCAG 2.2 AA — editorial doesn't excuse low contrast.
- Dark mode should feel intentional, not inverted.
