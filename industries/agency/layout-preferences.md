# Agency — Layout Preferences

## Recommended structure
1. Editorial statement + one signature visual
2. Selected work (large, curated)
3. Capabilities in narrative form (not icon grid)
4. Culture/team point of view
5. Recognition or context (press, clients) when it supports positioning
6. Direct contact section

## Layout rules
- Asymmetry and negative space over grid uniformity.
- Break sections with typography or imagery, not decorative dividers.
- Case study previews earn full-bleed treatment.
- Mobile keeps editorial rhythm; do not collapse to generic cards.
