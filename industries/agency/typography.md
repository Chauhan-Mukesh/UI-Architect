# Agency — Typography

## Typographic style
- Tone target: distinctive, confident, editorial.
- A display face can carry personality; pair with a highly legible text face.
- Type is a signature of positioning; commit hard.

## Readability rules
- Display can push weight/spacing further than most industries — but body copy stays 60–75ch.
- Avoid gimmicky variable-font stunts unless they advance the narrative.
- Ensure all-caps and letter-spaced treatments still pass legibility on small screens.
