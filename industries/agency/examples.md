# Agency — Example Direction

## Example page goals
- Communicate creative point of view and prove capability through selected work.
- Filter for fit before contact rather than maximizing form fills.

## Suggested example scenario
- Landing page with editorial hero, 4–6 case studies, services capability block, team, contact form.
- Layout should feel bespoke; avoid template rhythms.

## Review checklist
- Does the visual language match the positioning?
- Is the work the star, or has copy taken over?
- Would a mismatched prospect self-select out here?
