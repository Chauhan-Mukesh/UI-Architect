# Agency — Best Practices

## UX priorities
- Design for prospects evaluating creative or strategic capability, not commodity buyers.
- Lead with distinctive work; delay marketing copy until credibility is established.
- Make the contact path frictionless — prospects arrive after they've decided to explore.

## Content strategy
- Case studies over feature bullets. Show outcome, role, and method.
- Voice must match the work: an agency selling boldness cannot present cautiously.
- Every page should answer "can they do this for us?" within the first fold.

## Interaction guidance
- Portfolio interactions favor exploration; keep hover/preview states meaningful.
- One clear contact CTA per page — never dilute with newsletter/download secondary CTAs.
- Motion is a signature; use it deliberately, not decoratively.
