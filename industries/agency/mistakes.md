# Agency — Common Mistakes

- Templated portfolio grids that make distinctive work look interchangeable.
- Loud "we do everything" service lists that dilute positioning.
- Stock photography where real team or work should appear.
- Motion effects that draw attention to the site instead of the work.
- Contact forms that treat leads as commodity rather than qualifying fit.

## Specific risk
- Copying a well-known agency's aesthetic — buyers who know the reference will devalue the work.
