# Agency — Conversion Goals

## Primary conversion
- Target: qualified inbound (brief submissions, discovery calls).
- Primary CTA: "Start a project" / "Send a brief".

## Secondary conversion
- Newsletter or long-form insight subscription for nurturing.
- Press/careers path for adjacent audiences (talent, media).

## KPI orientation
- Brief submissions with quality signals (budget, scope filled)
- Case study read-through rate
- Time on site (proxy for portfolio engagement)
- Return visits before contact
