# Typography — Minimal

## Purpose
Typographic voice: **calm, quiet, precise**.

## Pairing
- Display: Neutral geometric or humanist sans.
- Body: Same family as display, lower weight for body.

## Rules
- Restrict weights: regular + semibold
- Rely on scale and rhythm, not decoration
- Line-heights on the generous side (1.6+)
- Body copy stays at 60–75ch measure.
- Use tabular numerals for tables and data.
- Fallback stack matches metrics of the primary.

## Anti-patterns
- Multiple accent weights
- Decorative italics
- All-caps body copy.
- Ornamental script for anything beyond a headline.

## When to choose this voice
- Match against the tone adjectives in the design direction card.
- Cross-check with the industry recommendation before committing.
