# Typography — Startup

## Purpose
Typographic voice: **confident, distinct, current**.

## Pairing
- Display: Distinctive geometric or humanist sans.
- Body: Same family or a warmer humanist sans.

## Rules
- Weight contrast is the primary hierarchy tool
- Variable font support enables tight scaling
- Match motion posture (see animations/principles)
- Body copy stays at 60–75ch measure.
- Use tabular numerals for tables and data.
- Fallback stack matches metrics of the primary.

## Anti-patterns
- Every trend at once
- Handwritten script for headlines
- All-caps body copy.
- Ornamental script for anything beyond a headline.

## When to choose this voice
- Match against the tone adjectives in the design direction card.
- Cross-check with the industry recommendation before committing.
