# Typography — Medical

## Purpose
Typographic voice: **calm, authoritative, accessible**.

## Pairing
- Display: Humanist sans with generous curves.
- Body: Same family at comfortable body size.

## Rules
- Larger base size (17–18px) for older audiences
- Strong contrast on form labels and dosages
- Avoid narrow tracking on legal and dosage text
- Body copy stays at 60–75ch measure.
- Use tabular numerals for tables and data.
- Fallback stack matches metrics of the primary.

## Anti-patterns
- Trendy variable-weight display
- Thin weights on critical information
- All-caps body copy.
- Ornamental script for anything beyond a headline.

## When to choose this voice
- Match against the tone adjectives in the design direction card.
- Cross-check with the industry recommendation before committing.
