# Typography — Government

## Purpose
Typographic voice: **accessible, plain, patient**.

## Pairing
- Display: Practical humanist sans (open-license preferred).
- Body: Same family; ensure Cyrillic/Latin extended coverage if audience requires.

## Rules
- Meet WCAG 2.2 AA at minimum, aim for AAA on body text
- Prefer open-license faces (public accountability)
- Avoid ornamental display
- Body copy stays at 60–75ch measure.
- Use tabular numerals for tables and data.
- Fallback stack matches metrics of the primary.

## Anti-patterns
- Marketing-flavored display
- Multiple accent colors on typography
- All-caps body copy.
- Ornamental script for anything beyond a headline.

## When to choose this voice
- Match against the tone adjectives in the design direction card.
- Cross-check with the industry recommendation before committing.
