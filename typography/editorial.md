# Typography — Editorial

## Purpose
Typographic voice: **editorial, considered, publication-grade**.

## Pairing
- Display: Serif display (transitional or modern) with strong italic support.
- Body: Serif or high-quality humanist sans for body.

## Rules
- Wider measure allowed for immersive reading (up to 80ch)
- Drop caps and pull quotes are appropriate
- Body text 17–19px for long-form
- Body copy stays at 60–75ch measure.
- Use tabular numerals for tables and data.
- Fallback stack matches metrics of the primary.

## Anti-patterns
- Trendy geometric sans for a magazine feel
- All-caps body text
- All-caps body copy.
- Ornamental script for anything beyond a headline.

## When to choose this voice
- Match against the tone adjectives in the design direction card.
- Cross-check with the industry recommendation before committing.
