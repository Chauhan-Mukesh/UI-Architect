# Typography — Corporate

## Purpose
Typographic voice: **trustworthy, established, legible**.

## Pairing
- Display: Humanist sans or transitional serif.
- Body: System-safe body face with excellent legibility at small sizes.

## Rules
- Consistent hierarchy; avoid mixing display styles
- Numbers tabular in tables and dashboards
- Ensure legibility on standard corporate output (PDF export, print)
- Body copy stays at 60–75ch measure.
- Use tabular numerals for tables and data.
- Fallback stack matches metrics of the primary.

## Anti-patterns
- Playful display faces that undercut authority
- Ultra-light weights for anything but display
- All-caps body copy.
- Ornamental script for anything beyond a headline.

## When to choose this voice
- Match against the tone adjectives in the design direction card.
- Cross-check with the industry recommendation before committing.
