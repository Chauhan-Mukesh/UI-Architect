# Conversion Optimization

## Objective
Increase completion of the primary business action while preserving trust.

## Priorities
- Clarify value proposition above the fold.
- Reduce friction in high-intent flows.
- Place proof near commitment points.
- Remove unnecessary fields and steps.
- Reinforce confidence post-conversion.

## Metrics orientation
- Primary conversion rate
- Step-level drop-off
- CTA click-through quality
- Form completion success
